About The Tenacious Ant

The foundation of The Tenacious Ant was built on a simple philosophy: hardware should be as resilient and hardworking as nature's greatest engineers. We are a small team of dedicated IT professionals and hardware enthusiasts located out of Charlotte NC, who treat every motherboard, GPU, and processor as a critical piece of a larger ecosystem.

We do not just snap parts together. We meticulously route cables, optimize airflow, and fine-tune bios settings to extract every drop of performance. Our tenacity ensures that when you boot up a system built by us, you are experiencing computing perfection engineered by a colony that cares.
