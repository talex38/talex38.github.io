Website Evaluation

Apple

  • Contrast: Uses high-contrast color schemes, such as dark text on a clean white background or stark white text on a dark background. This makes all content highly legible and accessible.
  • Repetition: Use the same clean sans-serif font (San Francisco), consistent button styles, and identical navigation structures across all pages to create a unified and predictable user experience.
  • Alignment: Relies on a strict grid system. Product images, headings, and paragraphs are perfectly aligned, creating a sharp, professional look that guides the user's eye smoothly down the page.
  • Proximity: Related elements—like a product's name, its short tagline, and the "Buy" button—are grouped closely together and separated from other content by generous amounts of white space.

Ling's Cars

  • Contrast: Site frequently uses clashing neon colors layered over busy, patterned backgrounds. This creates a visually overwhelming experience and makes much of the text incredibly difficult to read.
  • Repetition: The site repeats distracting elements like animated GIFs and utilizes dozens of different fonts, colors, and border styles on a single page.
  • Alignment: Elements are placed haphazardly without a coherent grid. Text, images, and videos overlap or sit out of alignment with one another, making it hard to figure out where to look next.
  • Proximity: The layout is incredibly cluttered. Unrelated elements—like car lease deals, browser games, and random flashing banners—are jammed closely together, destroying any sense of logical visual organization.
